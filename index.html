Recommendations

● P1  Workout launch friction: 76% of workout exits happen before 20% completion. Investigate the first section's onboarding/audio/loading — this is the single biggest leak.
● P2  New-install day-1 churn (91%): the first session isn't converting installers into returners. Strengthen the first-run hook and a day-1 re-engagement push.
● P3  Social/medals underused (3–4 users): either surface them more prominently or de-prioritize. The ~24% paywall conversion is healthy — protect it.
